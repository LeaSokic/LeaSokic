Lea Sokić

Moje ime je Lea Sokić te pohađam Grafički fakultet u Zagrebu. Imam 19 godina i prebivam u studentskom naselju Stjepan Radić. U slobodno vrijeme bavim se crtanjem. Za kućne ljubimce imam 3 mačke i jednog psa.

Citat:

It was many and many a year ago,
In a kingdom by the sea,
That a maiden there lived whom you may know
And this maiden she lived with no other thought
Than to love and be loved by me.
